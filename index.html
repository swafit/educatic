About Us

Edducatic.site is a website that raises awareness to the lack of clean water in the Native American territories around Canada. We are a group of activists that are attempting to give as much attention to this problem as possible. We believe in the fact of having equal rights and equal access to basic necessities, no matter what gender, religion or financial background someone comes from.
